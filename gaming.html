Video Games

Oh boy, where do I even start?

Video games have been a huge part of my life for as long as I can remember. I have been playing video games since I was around 6 years old, and I have been hooked ever since.

The first video game I ever played was Need for Speed: Most Wanted because my older brother was playing it at the time and I decided to (very stealthily) try it out when he wasn't at home.


And ever since those first experiences, video games have always been something I hold near and dear to my heart. No matter how frustrating some of them might be. Hell, some of my most fond memories from my childhood are just peacefully playing a Nintendo Wii on a random weekend.

The last couple of years my taste in games has been pretty much the same. Games that I have been playing for multiple years now include:

  • League of Legends (unfortunately)
  • Various Battle Royale games like Apex Legends
  • The Counter-Strike series
  • And multiple Sports game titles
